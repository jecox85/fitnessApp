About

This contents of this exercise program were found on the r/bodyweightfitness subreddit on reddit.com and it outlined here.

This routine is intended as a full-body exercise routine done with minimal equipment and is intended to be done no more than three times a week with a minimum of one rest day between workout days.

How Progressions Work

Athletes should choose a progression difficulty based on their current fitness level. They should start with 3 sets of 5 reps of that progression with good form and should only move to the next progression when they are able to perform 3 sets of 8 reps.

If the exercise is a static hold, the athlete should start progressions at 10-30 seconds and should only move to the next progression when they can hold 30 seconds for all 3 sets.
